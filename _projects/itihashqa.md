## RAG for Bangladeshi History

ItihashQA is a Bangladeshi history-focused question-answering system built with retrieval-augmented generation and large language models. The project emphasizes grounded retrieval to reduce hallucination.

### Focus

- Retrieval-augmented generation.
- LLM-based question answering.
- Historical knowledge grounding and evaluation.
