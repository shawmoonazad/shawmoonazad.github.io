## Quantum Key Distribution Simulations

Implemented BB84, B92, and E91 quantum key distribution protocols using IBM Qiskit. The suite includes circuit construction, error analysis, eavesdropping detection, and visualization of quantum state behavior.

### Focus

- Qiskit implementation of canonical QKD protocols.
- Eavesdropping detection and error-rate analysis.
- Educational and research-oriented simulation workflows.
