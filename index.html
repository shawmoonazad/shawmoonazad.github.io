My Research Works in Brief

Quantum Computing Research

[1] Enhancing the security of image transmission in the Quantum era: A Chaos-Assisted QKD Approach using entanglement.

TL;DR:
Combining the E91 QKD protocol with logistic chaotic maps, this study develops a secure image transmission scheme that detects eavesdropping using entanglement tests and chaos encryption. Simulations confirm enhanced encryption efficiency.

Research Methodology:

[2] Multi-Layered Security System: Integrating Quantum Key Distribution with Classical Cryptography to Enhance Steganographic Security.

TL;DR:
This research merges QKD with traditional encryption (using SHA and AES) to secure steganographic images, showing high randomness and resilience against differential attacks.

[1] SAD: Self-assessment of depression for Bangladeshi university students using machine learning and NLP.

TL;DR:
