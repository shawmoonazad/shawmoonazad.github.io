Hi! I'm Md Shawmoon Azad, a researcher exploring the intersection of Quantum Security × AI. I'm currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Cleveland State University under the supervision of Dr. Sathish A. P. Kumar, where I work on Hybrid Post-Quantum TLS frameworks for quantum-safe cloud communication.

Previously, I was a Research Assistant at the Optics-Quantum & AI Lab of North South University, supervised by Dr. Mahdy Rahman Chowdhury and I have also closely worked with Dr. Sifat Momen.

Research Interests: (I) NIST-Aligned Hybrid Quantum–Classical Cryptography, (II) Post-Quantum Communication Protocols, (III) Efficient Quantum Machine Learning (QML) for Constrained Hardware, (IV) Translational Quantum × AI Applications.
