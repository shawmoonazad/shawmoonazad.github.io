teaching

Teaching, instruction, and research mentorship.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe strong technical teaching connects fundamentals with working systems. My approach emphasizes project-based learning, reproducible coding habits, debugging confidence, and the link between theory and implementation. In research mentorship, I guide students through the complete research lifecycle: literature review, problem formulation, implementation, evaluation, writing, and presentation.
