Research Interests

Bridging Quantum Security and AI

Hybrid Post Quantum Cryptography Algorithms

To designe hybrid Post-Quantum Cryptography frameworks to achieve quantum-safe communication. My work integrates NIST-standardized schemes (Kyber, Dilithium) into robust TLS/IPsec pipelines with improved policy engine for secure cloud infrastructures.

PQC Hybrid Cryptography Kyber Dilithium TLS 1.3

Secure Quantum Communication Protocols

To develope Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) protocols integrated with classical encryption layers for imperfect quantum channels. Focus areas include entanglement-based QKD using CHSH inequalities to evesdrop detection.

QKD BB84 E91 Entanglement CHSH Inequality

Efficient Quantum Machine Learning (QML)

To explore resource-efficient Quantum Machine Learning algorithms for constrained hardware by optimizing parameterized quantum circuits and exploring variational hybrid networks. The goal is to enable low-depth, high-fidelity QML models deployable on NISQ-era devices.

QML Variational Circuits Quantum Neural Networks NISQ Optimization
